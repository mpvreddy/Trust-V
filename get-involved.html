Get Involved

WE HAVE MANY WAYS FOR YOU TO HELP US BREAK THE CYCLE OF POVERTY AND EMPOWER THE WOMEN. WHETHER BY DONATING YOUR TIME, MONEY, OR SKILLS, THERE ARE PLENTY OF OPPORTUNITIES FOR PEOPLE LIKE YOU WHO WANT TO MAKE A DIFFERENCE!

DONATE

We are grateful for the donations we receive from people who give to Sambhali Trust. They help us do the work that we do. We thank you in advance for anything you can give to support us, because it will help change lives.

VOLUNTEER

Committed volunteers play an essential role in our many programmes. We have had volunteers of all ages share their skills with the beneficiaries of the Trust. As a volunteer, you get an insight into the intricacies of Indian culture and develop an understanding of how a grassroots non-profit charitable organisation operates and help change the lives of hundreds of women and girls.

PARTNER WITH US

Join us in our mission to create positive change in the lives of women and girls. Your partnership is vital in helping us expand our reach and impact. Together, we can make a real difference.

International Collaboration

Sahasra Charitable Trust values partnerships with international organizations dedicated to social progress. Through collaboration, we amplify our impact, exchange knowledge, and implement best practices to empower women and girls globally. Join us in fostering meaningful change on a global scale.

SHARE OUR WORK AND JOIN THE CONVERSATION

We have a lot to say about women's rights, and we think you do too.

Follow us on social media and sign up for our newsletter to stay informed and help us spread the word.

The Sahasra Charitable Trust is committed to improving the lives of underprivileged communities through various educational, Food Distribution, and empowerment initiatives. With a focus on sustainable development and holistic support, we strive to create lasting positive change in the areas we serve.
