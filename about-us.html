Overview

At our trust, we believe in the transformative power of compassion and action. With a steadfast commitment to serving communities in need, we embark on a journey to make a meaningful difference in the lives of individuals and families.

Through initiatives ranging from education and healthcare to disaster relief and livelihood programs, we strive to address pressing issues and empower marginalized populations. Our approach is rooted in empathy, integrity, and collaboration, as we work hand in hand with communities to build a brighter, more resilient future for all.

In our Empowerment Centres, we provide underprivileged Rajasthani women and girls an education in English, Hindi, and Maths.

Through our Scholarship Project and Boarding Homes, we sponsor children from poverty-stricken families to continue going to school.

We provide counseling, health, and legal support when women are abused, abandoned, and blackmailed.

Most importantly, we provide a space that is safe and free of discrimination, to uplift the hearts and voices of deprived Rajasthani women and nurture lasting networks of support.

The Sahasra Charitable Trust is committed to improving the lives of underprivileged communities through various educational, Food Distribution, and empowerment initiatives. With a focus on sustainable development and holistic support, we strive to create lasting positive change in the areas we serve.
